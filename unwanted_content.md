## Topics

Unfriendly, aggressive, or disrespectful language, including insults or personal attacks

Hate speech or any form of discriminatory language

Judgmental or derogatory comments about personal taste, such as labeling things as “tacky,” “low-class,” or similar expressions

Content related to academic elitism or employment-based comparison or anxiety, including discussions centered around prestige hierarchies

Mentions of or emphasis on keywords such as “985” or “211” in an evaluative or comparative context

Gossip, conflicts, or “fan wars” in the entertainment industry, especially content that involves comparisons, disparagement, or “putting others down” narratives

Please filter out or deprioritize content matching the above characteristics.
